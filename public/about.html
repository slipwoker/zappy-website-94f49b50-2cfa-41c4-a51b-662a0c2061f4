About Olga

Olga's Story

From her earliest years, Olga was drawn to the night sky — not simply as a canvas of stars, but as a living language. What began as a childhood fascination with constellations quietly deepened into a lifelong study of the cosmos and its relationship to human experience. Long before she became theastrologerher clients trust today, she was a devoted student of ancient traditions, spending years immersed in classical texts, Hellenistic techniques, and the symbolic grammar of the planets.

Her path into professional practice was gradual and intentional. She trained across multiple astrological traditions — natal, predictive, and synastry — building a layered understanding that honours the depth of the craft. Over time, her work evolved from personal curiosity into a disciplined practice grounded in both celestial study and genuine care for the people who sought her guidance.

What distinguishes Olga's approach is her belief that astrology should be both spiritually resonant and practically useful. A birth chart reading ordaily horoscopeconsultation with her is never abstract or fatalistic — it is a thoughtful conversation about timing, temperament, and the choices available to you. She brings the same rigour to a transit forecast as she does to a soul-level natal reading.

Today, Olga works with clients across the world, offering guidance rooted in honesty, nuance, and a deep reverence for the cosmos. Whether you are navigating a major life transition, seeking clarity on relationships, or simply curious about your celestial blueprint, her practice offers a space where ancient wisdom meets present-day life.
